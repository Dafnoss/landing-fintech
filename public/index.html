FAQ

Questions & Answers

Clients ask us questions regularly. We decided to be proactive and prepared a few answers before you’d even asked.

How do I ensure I get quality software?
With more than 16 years of experience, we developed 50+ Fintech solutions. 12 million people all around the globe use our solutions every day. A-class Fintech companies already trust us. You also get a 12-month warranty on the software we develop.
Should I get a custom or a ready-made solution?
Ready-made software products can be a good solution for standard tasks, typical for a wide range of companies, but when it comes to your specific needs, finding a perfect match out of the box may be a challenge. And if you want to leverage the most modern and innovative technologies to be ahead of competitors, custom development is the best choice.
Do you sign an NDA?
Yes, we do. We always care about information security and send you an NDA if you don’t have a form ready to sign. We use internal servers to run and test your project and never share your private information with a 3rd party.
How long does it take to evaluate my project?
It all depends on the requirements. Usually, we are able to provide an initial rough estimate even based on a high-level description of the business requirements, and it takes 2-4 days. For more accurate estimation we perform business analysis and prepare necessary specifications and wire-frames — this process can take a few weeks.
Do you deal with financial regulation compliance?
Yes, we do. Since some Fintech niches may be strictly regulated by international and local laws, we have professional financial and banking consultants working side by side with our developers.
How long does it take to develop fully-functional software?
The whole process takes about 2 months: from design and analysis to launching a first working prototype. However, a final release of a fully functional solution depends on the technical requirements, product features, and changes appearing in the development process.
